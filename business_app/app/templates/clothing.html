Types of Business Attire

Casual Dress:

  • Jeans, shorts, t-shirts, sandals, and athletic shoes allowed
  • Clothing with words, terms, or pictures that may be offensive is unacceptable
  • Frayed and dirty clothing is unacceptable

Business Casual Dress:

  • Jeans discouraged, unless it is a designated dress down day
  • Shorts, sandals, t-shirts, sundresses, and tank tops are not allowed
  • Short or long sleeve shirt with collars
  • Khakis or corduroys
  • Vests and sweaters
  • Casual shoes, but no athletic shoes

Business Formal Dress:

  • Suits or jackets with dress shirts, ties, formal leather-like shoes
  • Skirt suits or pant suits
  • Stockings and closed toe leather heel shoes

Clothing Store Suggestions:

  • Express
  • Old Navy
  • Banana Republic
  • J Crew
  • Nordstrom
