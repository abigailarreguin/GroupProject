We are InterviewIQ! A group of students coming together to help you succeed

This webapp is our Software Engineering project at FSU, we came up with the idea because there is no easy way to organize interviews, meeting with potential employers, or the numerous career fairs offered.

We wanted to give students/recent grads a way to organize career events and also have a source that will help the user with what to wear to these events, and also an FAQ feature.

Creators of InterviewIQ: Holly Culver, Hunter Davis, Elsie Adu, Abigail Perry, Abigail Esler
